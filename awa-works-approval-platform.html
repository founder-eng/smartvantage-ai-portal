Manual for Aerodrome Works — the Umbrella

The governing manual that sits above and integrates the six SOPs of the Works Approval process — establishing how aerodrome works are submitted, endorsed, notified, inspected and handed over.

Reference
ADA/COO/AC&WA/AWA/MAN/001

Senior Manager, AWA
Classification
Official Use

Purpose & Scope

The Manual establishes the mandatory framework by which any Aerodrome Works — projects, planned or corrective maintenance, inspections, surveys or changes to infrastructure within or affecting the airside operational area — are governed, endorsed via the AWAC, notified via AWAN, de-conflicted via the Lookahead and Daily Conference Call, and verified via Site Inspection.

Applicable aerodromes: OMAA, OMAL, OMAD, OMBY, OMDL.
Regulatory basis: UAE GCAA CAR-AGA Part-ADR, Part-ACM, Part-VFI, Part-TRG.

The 6-SOP Sequence

1
AWAC SOP (SOP/100)

AWAC Pack submitted & auto-filtered; accepted packs auto-compiled into the agenda; Teams meeting; AI-generated MOM; endorsement issues an AWAN.

2
AWAN SOP (SOP/AWAN)

On endorsement the AWAN is generated; the register and satellite map update automatically.

3
Site Inspection SOP (SOP/102)

Inspection scheduled against the newly released AWAN.

4
Vehicle Management SOP (SOP/120)

Vehicle condition checked; AVP mandatory before airside access.

5
Daily Conference Call SOP (SOP/101)

Any non-compliance announced; AI-generated MOM from transcript & attendance.

6
Handover SOP (SOP/103)

On completion the Project Team hands over the area for inspection and closure.

Definition of Key Terms

Standard Operating Procedures (1–6)

The six governing SOPs of the Works Approval process, in sequence under the Manual umbrella. Select a procedure to view its lifecycle, platform automation, roles and regulatory basis.

Controlled Templates & Forms

Standardised forms supporting each procedure. Catalogue with originating SOP and form reference.

Regulatory Source Hierarchy

The four-tier hierarchy applied consistently across all works-approval decisions. Lower tiers apply only where higher tiers are silent.

Tier 1 — Internal SOP
Aerodrome Manual & SOPs for the specific aerodrome. Always applied first.
Tier 2 — GCAA CAR-AGA
Parts ADR, ACM, VFI, TRG. Governs where the SOP is silent or less stringent.
Tier 3 — ICAO
Annex 14, Docs 9157 / 9774 / 9859 / 9137 where Tiers 1–2 are silent.
Tier 4 — EASA CS-ADR-DSN
Residual reference only.

Key Regulatory Anchors

ADR.OPS.B.070 — Aerodrome works safety (PRIMARY). Governs the conduct of works and the protection of the movement area.
ADR.OR.H.065 / H.070 — Other approvals & changes. Triggered by pavement-type substitution and other material changes; three sequential GCAA gates (RDR no-objection → technical acceptance → operational approval).
AMC1 ADR.OPS.C.013 — Rigid-pavement overload margin is 10% (UAE provision supersedes the legacy ICAO 5%).
ADR.OPS.A.057(b)(13) — Conditional availability NOTAM. Designates conditional availability, not a runway closure.
ADR.OPS.B.045 — Low-visibility procedures / LVO safeguarding during airside works.
